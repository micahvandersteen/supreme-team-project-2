This dashboard presents data on Minnesota's approximately 300 public school districts. Interactive plots show the relationships between reading proficiency and factors such as spending and student population size. Hover over the data points below to see District-specific information.The interactive map page depicts the boundaries of Minnesota's districts for the 2018-19 school year. Click on a district to see its name and the average number of students enrolled. Activate additional map layers like Median Household Income by District.

Source Data

Data used for the analyses were sourced from the U.S. Census, the Minnesota Department of Education, and the National Center for Education Statistics (NCES). For more information on the data sources see the links provided below.

  • 2018-Minnesota Median Household Income Data. American Community Survey Table S1901. Retrieved from https://data.census.gov/cedsci/table?q=Minnesota-income.gov
  • National Center for Education Statistics (NCES). School District Boundaries-Current. Retrieved from https://data-nces.opendata.arcgis.com/datasets/school-district-boundaries
  • 2018-2019 General Fund Expenditures (Minnesota Department of Education (MDE). General Fund unreserved Balance for FY 2013-Present. Downloaded 4/4/2020 from https://public.education.mn.gov/MDEAnalytics/DataTopic.jsp?TOPICID=43
  • 2019 Average Daily Membership (Minnesota Department of Education (MDE). Downloaded 4/4/2020 from https://public.education.mn.gov/MDEAnalytics/DataTopic.jsp?TOPICID=2
  • 2019 Percent Proficient in Reading (Minnesota Department of Education (MDE). Reading MCA MTAS Results. Downloaded 4/4/2020 from https://public.education.mn.gov/MDEAnalytics/DataTopic.jsp?TOPICID=1
  • 2019 Average Teacher Salary (Minnesota Professional Educator Licensing and Standards Board (PELSB). Licensed Average Salary by District. Downloaded 4/4/2020 from https://public.education.mn.gov/MDEAnalytics/PELSB.jsp?TOPICID=437
