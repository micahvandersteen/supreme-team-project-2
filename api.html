Looking for Minnesota education data?

Use the base url to call for education data. Available variables are in the table below.

Base URL = "https://api.MN_ED_Dashboard.io"

Variables
Variable Description
GeoID Seven digit geographic identifier number used to reference the geographic area of the district.
ORGID Twelve digit organization ID number used by the state of Minnesota to reference the district.
District District name.
ADM Average Daily Membership (ADM) for grades EC through 12. ADM represents the portion of the year that a student is enrolled in the district.
GeneralFundExpend The district's gereral fund expenditures.
PerPupilExpend The district's per pupil expenditures. The per pupil expenditures are calcualted by dividing the general fund expenditures by the average daily membership.
AvgTeachSalary Average salary for licensed teachers working in the district.
ReadingProficiency The percent of students scoring proficient in reading on the Statewide Academic Accountability Tests. The percent proficient is calculated by dividing the count of students who meet or exceed standards by the total tested.
TotalEnrolled Total Number of Students Enrolled.
Female_Percent Percent of female students enrolled at the district.
Male_Percent Percent of male students enrolled at the district.
AIAN_Percent Percent of students enrolled who identified as American Indian or Alaska Native (AIAN).
Asian_Percent Percent of students enrolled who identified as Asian.
NHPI_Percent Percent of students enrolled who identified as Native Hawaiian and other Pacific Islander (NHPI).
Hispanic_Percent Percent of students enrolled who identified as Hispanic or Latino.
Black_Percent Percent of students enrolled who identified as Black or African American.
White_Percent Percent of students enrolled who identified as White.
TwoMore_Percent Percent of students enrolled who identifed as Two or More Races.
Meals_Percent Percent of students enrolled who are receiving free/reduced priced meals.
MedianHouseIncome Median household income of households within the district's boundaries.
MHI_MarginofError Margin of error calculation for median household income.
NoComputer_Count The number of households within district boundaries that specified "No" to "Computer".
NoComputer_Percent The percent of households within district boundaries that specified "No" to "Computer".
YesComputer_Count The number of households within district boundaries that specified "Yes" to "Computer".
YesComputer_Percent The percent of households within district boundaries that specified "Yes" to "Computer".
NoInternet_Count The number of households within district boundaries that specified "No" to "Internet".
NoInternet_Percent The percent of households within district boundaries that specified "No" to "Internet".
YesInternet_Count The number of households within district boundaries that specified "Yes" to "Internet".
YesInternet_Percent The percent of households within district boundaries that specified "Yes" to "Internet".

Longitude Longitude
